About Me

Originally from Michigan, I moved to Atlanta in the beginning of 2016 looking for a fresh start. (and an escape from the cold)

After 13 years as a Data Analyst, I became highly proficient at using Microsoft Excel, and really enjoyed getting to build custom spreadsheets that would help make other people's jobs a little easier.

In pursuit of this, I decided to trek down the path of becoming a web developer. My first step on this new journey was to enroll in an Immersive Full Stack Web Development program at DigitalCrafts in Atlanta.
