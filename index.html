About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer with a desire to create useful applications that benefit others.

Originally from Michigan, I moved to Atlanta in 2016 looking for a fresh start. My past experience includes Data Analysis, Marketing, Website QA Testing, and Video Editing. One of my favorite hobbies is recording and editing old video games into movies. (I’m a big fan of the Final Fantasy game series)

After 13 years as a Data Analyst, I became very good at using Microsoft Excel. I loved getting to build custom spreadsheets that would help make someone’s job a little easier. The joy and satisfaction from making something that helps others has led me to pursue a career in Web Development. To that end, I attended an Immersive Full Stack Web Development program at DigitalCrafts in Atlanta.
